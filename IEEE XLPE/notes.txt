- TD Stability is tan-delta stability
- Delta TD is differential tan-delta
- Mean TD is mean tan-delta

These three metrics follow IEEE 400.2-2013

- Partial Discharge was measured in pC according to IEC 60270.
- Neutral corrosion was measured by using time domain reflectometer (TDR) during cable inspection
